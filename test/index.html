Welcome to the Peters Research Group!

Studying ordered soft-matter systems using advanced simulation techniques!



We are working to facilitate nanoscale control by applying the fundamentals of block copolymer phase separation and polymer dynamics.

Advanced atomistic and coarse-grained simulation methods are used to understand and control ordered soft matter systems.

For more information, meet our members or check out our research projects and publications.
